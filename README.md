## üîë Key Insights (Executive Summary)
- **Clothing, Shoes, and Technology** consistently drive the majority of revenue across the analyzed period.
- **Revenue exhibits a stable seasonal pattern**, with recurring peaks in **July and October** (2021‚Äì2023).
- **Mall of Istanbul, Kanyon, and Metrocity** consistently rank among the top-performing malls, indicating revenue concentration in strategic locations.
- **Customers aged 50+** dominate transaction volume and show a strong preference for cash payments, while younger customers demonstrate higher adoption of digital payment methods.
- **Books and Souvenir** remain consistently underperforming categories, reflecting limited revenue contribution and narrower market appeal.
